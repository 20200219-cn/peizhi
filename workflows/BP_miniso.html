财务BP的工作及工作重点

What a Finance BP Does & What Matters Most

我认为的财务BP，既要懂财务，更要懂业务。他/她工作的核心是围绕着降本增效展开的。

A Finance BP must master finance and understand the business. The core is improving efficiency and reducing costs.

“降本增效”听起来空泛，但对实操的人来说非常落地。下面结合过往经历，谈谈预算在其中的指导作用。

“Efficiency & cost reduction” sounds vague, yet it’s very practical. Based on experience, here is how the budget guides the work.

财务BP工作的起点是预算。我会收到业务部门提交的销售收入、成本、费用和资本支出等预算。懂销售就是懂业务，因此先从销售预测说起。

Budgeting is the starting point. BPs receive revenue, cost, OPEX and CAPEX budgets. Sales forecasting comes first.

1. 预算制定阶段

1) Budget Formulation (Q4)

目标：明确经营目标。

Goal: Set clear operating targets.

销售的 base 多取自往年同期数据，并结合各区域经营计划、SKU 上新节奏、市场活动、节假日等进行增减。 同时考虑汇率、关税、气温、政治稳定性等宏观因素做出调整。

Use prior-year same-period data as the base, then adjust for regional plans, SKU launch cadence, marketing events and holidays, plus macro factors like FX, tariffs, weather, and political stability.

对销售预测影响首当其冲的是开店模式，门店收入来源可能包括：

Store model strongly affects sales. Revenue streams may include:

1）供货收入：向加盟方收取一定溢价；
2）加盟费：按销售额比例收取；
3）保证金与品牌使用费：与新店数量强相关，可作为预测参数，但在滚动预测中可比性较弱。

(1) Supply margin charged to franchisees;
(2) Franchise fees based on sales;
(3) Deposits & brand royalties tied to new-store openings—useful parameters but less comparable in rolling forecasts.

2. 月度执行与预测阶段

2) Monthly Execution & Rolling Forecast

目标：执行月度计划，收集实际数据，与预算对比并更新预测。

Goal: Execute plan, collect actuals, compare vs. budget, and update forecasts.

如果搭建单店销售模型，销售额可分解为“客流量 × 转化率 × 客单价”。在看总体销售的同时，单独观察客单价与转化率的波动并归因。

For a single-store model, Sales = Traffic × Conversion × Ticket Size. Track ticket size and conversion separately and attribute changes.

还可构建区域模型。以澳大利亚为例，墨尔本/悉尼/阿德莱德的增长与产业结构不同：

Build regional models as well. In Australia, Melbourne/Sydney/Adelaide differ in growth and industrial structure:

阿德莱德旅游属性强，夏季客流大；悉尼与墨尔本更依赖工业与服务业。季节性有助于做对的时点上架对的商品。当地白领与留学生偏好也会影响预测。

Adelaide is tourism-heavy (higher summer traffic); Sydney/Melbourne rely more on industry/services. Seasonality helps place the right SKUs at the right time. Local white-collar and student preferences matter too.

3. 业务支持与决策分析阶段

3) Business Support & Decision Analytics

目标：参与测算，评估回报。

Goal: Engage in modeling and evaluate returns.

产品结构是销售预测的主干。先看类目毛利率差异（家具、美妆个护、玩具盲盒、IP 联名、零食等）。盲盒/美妆毛利高但占比不一定大，需要看 GM% 与结构占比是否合理。

Product mix underpins forecasts. Category GM% varies (home, beauty & personal care, blind boxes, IP co-brands, snacks). High-GM categories may be small, so check GM% and mix together.

Actual vs Budget 是分析原则。GM% 过低可能源于促销；过高亦可能暗示定价偏离市场。需要对标竞品均价以支撑定价策略的判断。

“Actual vs Budget” is the rule. Low GM% may reflect heavy promos; too high could hint mispricing vs market. Benchmark peers to validate pricing strategy.

进一步挖掘 GM 与周转的关系。高毛利常伴随慢周转、现金压力大；高周转往往毛利低、现金压力小。 关键在于找到 GM 与周转的最优组合点，并关注 GMROI、现金转换周期等指标。

Explore GM vs turnover. High GM often means slower turnover and cash pressure; high turnover tends to lower GM but lighter cash burden. Find the sweet spot and track GMROI and cash conversion cycle.

当库存积压时，即使 GM% 高也应果断促销——前提是整体利润健康。若资金紧张，牺牲少许毛利换更好现金流亦是理性选择。

If inventory piles up, promote even with high GM%—provided total profit remains healthy. When cash is tight, sacrificing some margin for liquidity can be rational.

产品结构还包括价格区间与生命周期。

Mix also includes price bands and life cycle.

若利润主要贡献区间下移，可能是结构老化、价格敏感上升、或竞品更具性价比；如由促销驱动，要评估“以价换量”能否恢复。 若为成本上升，则需联动成本预算模块应对。

If profit-contributing bands shift downward, causes may include aging mix, higher price sensitivity, or stronger competitors. If promos drove it, test whether volume sticks. If costs rose, respond via cost budgeting.

每个产品都有生命周期，这个角度同样重要。

Every product has a life cycle—equally important.

导入期可建 ROI 模型（总收入、变动成本、固定投入、净收益）。若数月后 ROI 较差或动销低，应停止放量。 成长/成熟/衰退期亦可类比处理。

In the introduction phase, build an ROI model (revenue, variable cost, fixed investment, net profit). If ROI lags or sell-through is low after months, stop scaling. Treat growth/mature/decline similarly.
