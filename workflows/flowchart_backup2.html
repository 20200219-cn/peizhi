Tax Filing Flowcharts, Payment Timelines, and Checklists (Year-End 31 Dec)

Hong Kong - Profits Tax Filing Flow (YE: 31 Dec)
flowchart TD
    A["31 Dec - Financial Year End"] --> B["Prepare Financial Statements"]
    B --> C["Audit by CPA firm (Jan - Apr)"]
    C --> D["IRD issues Profits Tax Return (1 Apr)"]
    D --> E{"Company category?"}
    E -->|Normal| F["Filing deadline: 2 May"]
    E -->|Dec YE extension| G["Filing deadline: 15 Aug"]
    F --> H["Submit: Audited FS + Tax Computation + PTR"]
    G --> H
    H --> I["IRD review and Assessment (Sep - Dec)"]
    I --> J{"Any objection?"}
    J -->|Yes| K["File Notice of Objection within 1 month"]
    J -->|No| L["Settle Final Tax (Jan - Apr next year)"]
    L --> M["Next cycle begins"]
Payment timeline - Provisional and Final Profits Tax
flowchart LR
    T0["YA N (basis: year ended 31 Dec N-1)"] --> T1["Assessment issued: Sep - Dec N"]
    T1 --> T2["Provisional Profits Tax - 1st instalment ~ Jan N+1 (about 75 pct)"]
    T2 --> T3["Provisional Profits Tax - 2nd instalment ~ Apr N+1 (about 25 pct)"]
    T3 --> T4["Final Profits Tax settlement after assessment and set-off"]
    T4 --> T5["Provisional for YA N+1 will be re-assessed next cycle"]
Exact months and percentages may vary by IRD notice; for Dec YE, 1st and 2nd instalments commonly fall around Jan and Apr.
Required documents - forms and attachments
  • PTR: BIR51 (Profits Tax Return for corporations).
  • Supplementary forms: industry or specific schedules (e.g., loss, depreciation). Use latest IRD supplementary forms S-series if applicable.
  • Attachments: Audited financial statements, detailed tax computation, supporting schedules (loss c/f, depreciation allowances, related party notes).
  • Review points: YE cut-off consistency, intra-group transactions, offshore claims if any, TP documentation, prior-year provisional set-off.
Singapore - Corporate Income Tax Flow (YE: 31 Dec)
flowchart TD
    A1["31 Dec - Financial Year End (Basis Period ends)"] --> B1["Close books and prepare FS (Jan - Apr)"]
    B1 --> C1["File ECI within 3 months after YE (by 31 Mar)"]
    C1 --> D1{"Revenue <= 5M AND ECI = 0?"}
    D1 -->|Yes| E1["ECI waiver applies"]
    D1 -->|No| F1["Start GIRO for ECI installments (up to 10-12 months)"]
    B1 --> G1["ACRA compliance"]
    G1 --> G2["If AGM required: hold AGM by 30 Jun"]
    G2 --> G3["File Annual Return by 31 Jul"]
    G1 --> G4["If AGM dispensed: file Annual Return by 31 Jul"]
    B1 --> H1["IRAS Corporate Tax Return (Form C-S/C) - e-File by 30 Nov (YA next year)"]
    H1 --> I1["IRAS issues Notice of Assessment (May - Sep following year)"]
    I1 --> J1{"Object to assessment?"}
    J1 -->|Yes| K1["Lodge objection within 2 months"]
    J1 -->|No| L1["Pay tax by due date (GIRO or lump sum)"]
    L1 --> M1["Next cycle begins"]
    E1 --> H1
    F1 --> L1
Payment timeline - GIRO and final tax
flowchart LR
    S0["ECI filed by 31 Mar N"] --> S1["GIRO for ECI - monthly instalments start (up to 10-12 months within YA N)"]
    S1 --> S2["Form C-S/C filed by 30 Nov N+1 (for YA N+1)"]
    S2 --> S3["IRAS Notice of Assessment issued (approx May - Sep N+2 for YA N+1)"]
    S3 --> S4["Final tax GIRO - monthly instalments per IRAS plan or lump sum by due date"]
ECI GIRO covers estimated tax for the current YA; final tax after NOA may be adjusted to reflect actual chargeable income.
Required documents - forms and attachments
  • ECI: Estimated Chargeable Income filing via myTax Portal.
  • Corporate return: Form C-S or Form C (and relevant schedules) via myTax Portal.
  • ACRA: Annual Return on BizFile+, XBRL FS if required by size and type; AGM minutes or resolutions if applicable.
  • Attachments: Financial statements, tax computation, capital allowance schedule, loss c/f schedule, TP documentation where applicable.
  • Review points: ECI waiver conditions, partial tax exemption or start-up exemption eligibility, TP on cross-border, GST registration status if relevant.
Canada - Corporation Income Tax Flow (YE: 31 Dec)
flowchart TD
    A2["31 Dec - Tax Year End"] --> B2["Close books and prepare FS (Jan - Apr)"]
    B2 --> C2["Corporate tax instalments for current year (usually monthly Jan - Dec)"]
    B2 --> D2["File T2 Corporation Income Tax Return by 30 Jun (6 months after YE)"]
    B2 --> E2{"Balance-due date?"}
    E2 -->|Most corporations| F2["Pay balance by end of Feb (2 months after YE)"]
    E2 -->|Eligible CCPC| G2["Pay balance by end of Mar (3 months after YE)"]
    B2 --> H2["Payroll info returns: T4 and T5 slips by last day of Feb"]
    B2 --> I2["GST/HST return - frequency based; annual with 12/31 YE due by 31 Mar"]
    D2 --> J2["CRA Notice of Assessment (timing varies)"]
    J2 --> K2{"Object to assessment?"}
    K2 -->|Yes| L2["File Notice of Objection within 90 days"]
    K2 -->|No| M2["Remit any amounts due and keep records"]
    M2 --> N2["Next cycle begins"]
Payment timeline - monthly instalments and balance due
flowchart LR
    C0["Start of calendar year N"] --> C1["Monthly instalments Jan - Dec N (based on prior year or current estimate)"]
    C1 --> C2["Year-end 31 Dec N - finalise income and CCA"]
    C2 --> C3["Balance due: end of Feb N+1 (most corps) or end of Mar N+1 (eligible CCPC)"]
    C3 --> C4["T2 filing due by 30 Jun N+1 (6 months after YE)"]
Instalment requirement depends on prior-year or current-year tax thresholds; CCPCs with small business income may have a 3-month balance-due date.
Required documents - forms and attachments
  • T2: Corporation Income Tax Return with required schedules.
  • Key schedules: Schedule 1 (Net income for tax purposes), Schedule 8 (CCA), Schedule 50 (Shareholder information), GIFI Schedules 100 and 125 (Balance Sheet and Income Statement).
  • Slips and returns: T4 payroll, T5 investment income; GST/HST return (e.g., GST34) per frequency.
  • Attachments: Financial statements, tax computation, loss and CCA continuity, intercompany and TP support if applicable.
  • Review points: Small business deduction and associated corporations, provincial allocations (Schedule 5), non-resident withholding, SR&ED if any.
